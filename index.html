Collaboration & Students

I am always looking for visiting scholars, Postdocs, PhD students, (remote) interns, and visiting students.

I am always open to possible discussion and research collaboration with junior students. I value diversity and fresh perspectives in artificial intelligence research.

Students from underrepresented groups are strongly encouraged to reach out! If you are interested in working together, please contact me at lenny.lilei.cs@gmail.com.

Research Overview

Multi-modal Representation Learning: Focused on analyzing imagery, 3D point clouds, and contextual data; Human/Embodied representation learning.

3D Understanding & Generation: Representation learning for 3D point clouds and 2D images; Generative AI for 3D objects, scenes, and humans.

Current Priorities: Large multimodal/language models for generation and understanding; Embodied and Physical AI; Virtual humans; AIGC for 3D/video content.
