Research Overview研究方向

Large Multimodal & Language Models (LMMs/LLMs):大型多模态与语言模型： Exploring unified representations across text, vision, audio, and 3D modalities. The focus is on cross-modal alignment, complex reasoning, and advancing foundational model architectures for comprehensive understanding. 文本、视觉、音频和三维等多模态的统一表征，重点关注跨模态对齐、复杂推理，以及面向综合理解的基础模型架构。

Embodied & Physical AI:具身与物理人工智能： Investigating AI perception and interaction within physical environments. Key areas include human and robotic representation learning, sensorimotor control, and grounding physical commonsense in multimodal models. 物理环境中的感知与交互，涵盖人类与机器人表征学习、感知运动控制，以及多模态模型中的物理常识 grounding。

Understanding & Generation (AIGC):理解与生成（AIGC）： Advancing Generative AI to create high-fidelity and controllable content. Research covers the generation of images, complex 3D scenes, dynamic videos, and interactive virtual humans. 生成式人工智能在高保真、可控内容生成中的应用，研究内容包括图像、复杂三维场景、动态视频和交互式虚拟人生成。

Cognitive Intelligence:认知智能： Explore foundational multimodal models to advance cognitive intelligence. This includes cross-modal reasoning in healthcare, cognitive decision support, and simulation. 探索面向认知智能的多模态基础模型，包括医疗场景中的跨模态推理、认知决策支持与仿真。

Current Priorities:近期重点： Large multimodal/language models for generation and cognitive understanding; Embodied and Physical AI; AIGC for 3D/video/human content; and multimodal AI into advanced cognitive health applications. 生成与认知理解的多模态模型、具身与物理人工智能、三维/视频/人物内容生成，认知医疗AI健康等场景中的应用。
